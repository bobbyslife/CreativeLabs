Goals & objectives

For Nautilus Books to continue to grow it needs to effectively provide customers with the ability to easily purchase books.
Unfortunately, the existing web site lacks this functionality and is having a negative impact on customer experience and company growth.

Customers need to be able to:
  • Purchase books online
  • Quickly search the large selection of books
  • Pay for purchases using Pay Pal or credit cards
  • Create wish lists and trade books via a customer portal
  • Track shipping and view order history
